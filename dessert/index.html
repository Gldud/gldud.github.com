About

Hello, We are Gazelle, your health-conscious premium cake delivery service. Many people rush into buying an unhealthy, stale cake to celebrate an anniversary. Our mission is to let you enjoy our healthy, yummy cakes with your loved ones, family or friends in time for any celebrations.

  • Low fat
  • Less sodium
  • Gluten free
  • No white flour
  • All natural
  • Less sugar

how it works

You choose

You choose the date that you need one or more of our super special cakes.

You pick

You pick your cake from our low fat, gluten free and naturally sweetened cakes in our app.

We make & deliver

Your cake is prepared by our team of chefs, featuring a celebrity chef and wellness expert and delivered fresh.

You enjoy

You can enjoy our cakes during any special occasion, such as birthdays, celebrations, and parties.
